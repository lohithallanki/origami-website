Origami Heart ❤️

Follow these simple steps to fold a beautiful paper heart

Step 1

Start with a square piece of origami paper. I am using a 7×7 inches origami paper.

Step 2

Rotate the paper diagonally.

Step 3

Fold the paper in half by folding the top corner to the bottom corner.

Step 4

Unfold.

Step 5

Fold the right corner to the left corner.

Step 6

Unfold.

Step 7

Fold the top corner to the center.

Step 8

Fold the bottom corner to the top edge.

Step 9

Fold the bottom right edge to the center crease.

Step 10

Repeat the same step on the left side.

Step 11

Turn the model over.

Step 12

Fold the top corners down.

Step 13

Fold the side corners back.

Step 14

Now you know how to make an origami heart! Easy wasn’t it?
