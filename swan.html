Follow these simple steps to fold a graceful paper swan

Step 1

Start with the white side facing up in a diamond position. Fold diagonally and unfold.

Step 2

Fold the bottom edges to the centerline and turn the paper over.

Step 3

Fold the bottom edges to the centerline again.

Step 4

Fold the bottom corner up to meet the top corner.

Step 5

Fold the top flap corner down along the crease.

Step 6

Fold the model in half from left to right.

Step 7

Pull the neck upward and flatten.

Step 8

Lift the head, adjust angle, and flatten.
