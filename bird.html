Follow each step carefully to create a beautiful paper bird

Step 1: Lay Origami Paper in Front of You

Place your square origami paper flat on a table or smooth surface.

Step 2: Fold Diagonally

Fold the paper diagonally to form a triangle. Align corners neatly.

Step 3: Fold Again to Create a Crease

Fold the triangle in half to create a center crease, then unfold.

Step 4: Start Forming the Wings

Use the center crease as a guide and fold one side upward.

Step 5: Fold the Other Wing

Repeat the fold on the opposite side, keeping both wings even.

Step 6: Adjust the Shape

Check alignment and adjust folds to maintain symmetry.

Step 7: Make the Neck

Fold a small section of the top to start shaping the bird‚Äôs neck.

Step 8: Form the Beak

Fold the tip forward or backward to create the beak.

Step 9: Fold the Bird in Half

Fold the entire model in half along the center crease.

Step 10: Final Touch

Pull the neck up, spread the wings, and adjust for the final look.
